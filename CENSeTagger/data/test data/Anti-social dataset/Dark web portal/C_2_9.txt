Translation insha'Allah: " A 'taaghoot' is everything [by which] the 'abd [slave of Allah] goes beyond the limits [set by the divine legislation], in matters of worship, or in matters of following, or in matters of obedience. So a 'taaghoot' is anything, other than Allah or the Messenger, to which the people legislate/adjudicate, or [anything] that is worshipped besides Allah, or [anyone] that is followed without knowledge/guidance from Allah, or [anyone] that is obeyed in those matters where Allah [alone] should be obeyed. And these are the 'tawagheet' of this world. To the person who contemplates upon the people who follow them, he will see that the majority of them turn away from the worship of Allah the Most High, and turn to the worship of 'taaghoot', and they [turn away] from the obedience of the Messenger, sallallahu alayhi was salama, to the obedience of the 'taaghoot' and following it." (end quote) May Allah protect the Ummah from ALL these evil tawaagheet that are followed and obeyed these days............!
