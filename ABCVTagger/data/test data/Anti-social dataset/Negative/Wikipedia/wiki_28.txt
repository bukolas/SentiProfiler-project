Gideon Hillman Consulting

Gideon Hillman Consulting, established in 2004, is an ISO 9001:2008 accredited company for the Supply of Specialist Supply Chain and Logistics Consulting and a corporate member of UKWA (United Kingdom Warehousing Association) as well as being a recognised member practice of the Institute of Consulting.[1]

Growth and Rebranding

The company experienced high growth in 2010 and in March 2011 they underwent a rebranding to coincide with the new offices for their additional administration personnel.[2][3]
Key Clients

The company has worked with UK and EU manufacturers for logistics network design and implementation, warehouse design and optimisation, logistics outsourcing and tender development and management. They are most notable for their work with Computers Unlimited, the principle European distributor of Apple Inc products.
