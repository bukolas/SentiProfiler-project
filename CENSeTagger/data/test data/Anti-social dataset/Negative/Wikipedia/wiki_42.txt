Walcha Road Railway Station is a railway station on the Main North railway line in New South Wales, Australia. It opened in 1882[1] and serves the village of Walcha Road and the town of Walcha to the east. It is presently served twice daily by the Countrylink Xplorer service between Sydney and Armidale. Passengers for this station mostly alight to catch a coach to the main town of Walcha.
