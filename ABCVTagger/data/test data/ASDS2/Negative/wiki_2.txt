Mark Millar (born 23 February 1988) is a Scottish football player who currently plays for Falkirk in the Scottish First Division.

Club career

Millar played for Celtic and spent time on loan at Hungarian club Újpest before signing for Falkirk. On the 21st of September 2011, he scored a freekick in the 90th minute in the Scottish Communities League Cup against Rangers.[1]
International career

Millar played once for the Scotland national under-21 football team.
